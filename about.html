About UCMPL

Excellence in Manufacturing, Commitment to Quality

Our Story

Ultra Craft Molders Pvt. Ltd. (UCMPL) was established in 2015 with a vision to become a leading manufacturer and exporter of premium plastic packaging solutions. Based in Haridwar, Uttarakhand, we have grown from a small manufacturing unit to a globally recognized company serving customers across 20+ countries.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide high-quality plastic packaging solutions that meet international standards while maintaining competitive pricing. Today, we are proud to be one of India's most trusted plastic packaging manufacturers, with a strong focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, advanced machinery, and a team of skilled professionals, UCMPL has established itself as a reliable partner for businesses worldwide. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has earned us the trust of over 500 international clients.

Our Mission

To be the global leader in plastic packaging solutions by delivering innovative, high-quality products that exceed customer expectations. We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices, environmental responsibility, and continuous innovation to meet the evolving needs of our international clients.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and preferred manufacturer of plastic packaging solutions, recognized for our excellence in quality, innovation, and customer service. We envision a future where UCMPL products are the standard of choice for businesses across all continents.
