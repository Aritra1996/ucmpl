About Plastic Global

Excellence in Manufacturing, Commitment to Quality

Our Story

Ultra Craft Molders Pvt. Ltd. (Plastic Global) is one of the top manufacturers of packaging solutions for FMCG, Food & Beverages, Healthcare, Consumer Goods & Automotive. Based in Prem Rice Mill Compound, Bahadrabad, Haridwar, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the plastic packaging industry.

Our journey began with a vision to provide high-quality plastic packaging solutions that meet the diverse needs of various industries. Today, we are proud to serve clients across multiple sectors with our comprehensive range of products including plastic bottles, bottle caps, road barricades, HDPE solar floats, pallets, sleeves, boxes, and specialized packaging for pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and edible oils.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, advanced machinery, and a team of skilled professionals led by Managing Director Ajeet Kumar Jain, Plastic Global has established itself as a reliable partner for businesses worldwide. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has earned us the trust of prestigious clients including AG Industries, Heinz, VIP Industries, and many more.

Our Mission

To be the global leader in plastic packaging solutions by delivering innovative, high-quality products that exceed customer expectations. We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices, environmental responsibility, and continuous innovation to meet the evolving needs of our international clients.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted and preferred manufacturer of plastic packaging solutions, recognized for our excellence in quality, innovation, and customer service. We envision a future where Plastic Global products are the standard of choice for businesses across all continents.
