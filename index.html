About Us

Ultra Craft Molders Pvt. Ltd. came into inception in 2010 with the sole purpose, vision and mission of one man, Mr. Ajeet Kumar Jain to provide best packaging solutions. Ultra Craft Molders Pvt. Ltd. is one of the top manufacturers of packaging solutions for FMCG, Food and Beverages, Healthcare, Consumer Goods and Automotive components as well as OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturers) parts.

UCMPL is leading manufacturer of plastics Caps, Closures, Automotive Components and Bottles (HDPE, PP and Pet) (Injection and Blow Molding) located at Haridwar. UCMPL is a young and goal oriented manufacturing company, which is making a mark in the packaging industry by providing the best to the customers. We at UCMPL are converting ideas to reality by providing packaging solution that has appeal and durability. “Crafts Only Quality” the tagline of the company is embedded in each every person associated with the company and it is the very foundation on which the company runs.

At UCMPL we believe that “Change is the only Constant”, thereby keeping up with the pace of the market and adapting to new technology and practices helps us fulfill the customers needs.
