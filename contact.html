Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our products and services

Our minimum order quantity varies by product type. For standard products, we typically require a minimum of 10,000 pieces. For custom products, the MOQ depends on the complexity and tooling requirements. Contact our export team for specific MOQ details.

Our products are certified with ISO 9001:2015, FDA approval for food and pharmaceutical packaging, CE marking for European markets, and various other international certifications depending on the product type and destination country.

Standard products typically take 15-20 days for production and packaging. Custom products may take 4-6 weeks depending on tooling requirements. Shipping time varies by destination (7-15 days for most countries). We provide detailed timelines during the quotation process.

Yes, we specialize in custom packaging solutions. Our design team works closely with clients to develop products that meet specific requirements. We have in-house tooling capabilities and can create prototypes for approval before mass production.

We offer flexible payment terms including T/T (Telegraphic Transfer), L/C (Letter of Credit), and for established clients, we may offer credit terms. Payment terms are discussed during the quotation process based on order value and client relationship.

We have a comprehensive quality control system including incoming material inspection, in-process quality checks, final inspection, and pre-shipment inspection. We also provide quality certificates and can arrange third-party inspection if required by the client.
